📷📍 Camera & Location Consent

To use this portal for execution checks, we need your permission to:

  • Camera — to capture your live login selfie and execution photos. Photos are stored against your store for review.
  • Location (GPS) — to confirm you are physically at the store (geo-fence) when you submit.

No data is shared with third parties. A consent record (device + timestamp) is logged for audit. You can revoke anytime by clearing your browser data.

Privacy Note

What we collect: live selfie at login, execution photos, GPS coordinates at submission, your name, designation, emp code and store code.

Why: to verify on-site presence (geo-fence), attribute submissions to the right person/store, and let reviewers approve or ask for a re-do.

Camera & Location are used only inside this portal while you are submitting. Nothing runs in the background.

Retention: submissions are kept for operational review and may be purged periodically by Central Ops.

Your choice: declining camera/location means you cannot submit execution photos, which require on-site verification.

    Test Mode
    When ON, Store login works on desktop and GPS/geofence is bypassed — for testing only. Turn OFF before go-live.
    Test ModeOFF
    When ON: Store login works on desktop and GPS / geo-fence is bypassed — for testing only. The store header shows a TEST tag. Turn OFF before go-live so only on-site mobile submissions are accepted.
    Photo Match Threshold
    The live photo must score at least this % against the reference sample to be accepted. The scorer compares scene structure + colour, so a correct re-shot of the same display typically lands ~60–80%, while a wrong subject (e.g. a face) scores in single digits. Recommended: 60–65. Setting it to 90 will reject most genuine re-shots.
    Geo-fence Radius
    How close (in metres) a store must physically be to its mapped location to log in and capture photos. Larger = more lenient. Default: 300 m. Applies only when the store is geo-mapped in Store Master and Test Mode is OFF.
